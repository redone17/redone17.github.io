Dongdong Wei is now a PhD student in Department of Mechanical Engineering, University of Alberta. His research focus is intelligent fault diagnosis. Specfically, his PhD topic is to develop transfer learning to achive accurate early gear fault diagnosis under working condition changes.

He finished both his Engineering Bachelor and Master degrees in University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), 2015 and 2018.

Apart from work and research, Dongdong loves to go into beautiful Rocky Mountains for hiking in summer time and skiing during long winters of Alberta. He also enjoys music and played tenor roles in UofA Concert Choir and UESTC Choir.
