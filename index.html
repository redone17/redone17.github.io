Dongdong Wei is now a PhD candidate in Department of Mechanical Engineering, University of Alberta. His PhD topic is to develop machine learning methods for health condition monitoring of wind turbines. He finished both his Engineering Bachelor and Master degrees at University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), in 2015 and 2018.
